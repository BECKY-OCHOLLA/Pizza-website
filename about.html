OUR STORY

Bekita's Pizza is not your average pizzeria. Founded in 2000, Bekita's Pizza combines old world quality, values and work ethic with new world ambiance and award winning appeal. Al brought with it the traditions, passion for excellence and commitment to quality that makes us what we are today. After working in some of Kenya,s finest pizzerias for 10 years, Becky opened her first location in Nairobi, Kenya. Refusing to use frozen dough, conveyor ovens, canned vegetables, or many other shortcuts used by other restaurants, Bekita's began setting the standard for her award-winning pizza.

THE PIZZA

Today, Bekita's continues to make fresh dough daily — often twice daily — from scratch. Produce is delivered 6 days a week to ensure only the freshest ingredients possible. Bekita's only uses one hundred percent whole milk mozzarella grated daily on site and nothing but fresh toppings, as Bekita's knows there is no substitute for quality. While quality, handmade pizza created in the classic kenyan tradition is the cornerstone of Bekita's Pizza, the ambiance of the restaurant adds to its appeal. Casual and stylish, the restaurants are a warm take on a contemporary vibe. Sleekly designed with cool fixtures and unexpectedly elegant twists, Bekitas's is a place you can go for a glass of wine with friends or take the entire little league team out for a garden-veggie pizza. Come watch our skilled artisans as they hand stretch and toss dough while turning pies in a traditional brick oven.
